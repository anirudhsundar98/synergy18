Intro

Synergy , the annual symposium of the Mechanical Engineering department of NIT Trichy! Prepare to be amazed by a dazzling array of tech events, workshops, and for the very first time, fun and entertainment filled informal events! From events like Fix ‘em Up to Junkyard Wars, from workshops like design to automobile. Held at the Mechanical Department of NIT Trichy’s campus, Synergy promises an educational, exciting and entertainment filled three days, and assures that every person attending will leave with newly acquired knowledge and fond memories. Come and celebrate Mechanical Engineering at Synergy NITT.
